My dream is to become part of the multimedia industry where I can combine technology and creativity. Although I chose to pursue Information Technology, I realized that I do not have to leave that dream behind.

Being involved in live production, cameras, and digital media showed me how technology can be used to communicate messages and connect with people. This experience inspired me to continue developing my skills in multimedia and digital content creation.
